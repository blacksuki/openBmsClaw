## Source Type Guide

| Type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Official | Government, regulator, standards body, certification body |
| Company official | Official product page, manual, datasheet, certificate |
| User voice | Review, X post, forum post, interview |
| Industry secondary | Article, report, analyst commentary |
| Internal | Interview note, supplier quotation, meeting note |

## Confidence Guide

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| High | Primary source, directly supports the claim |
| Medium | Credible source, but indirect or incomplete |
| Low | Useful clue, not enough for conclusion |
